Chat+ is a massive overhaul to Terraria's chat system; making it look better, smarter and more fun with player icons, mod icons, emojis, images, links, a scrollbar, a better text editor, and more!

[h3] ✨ What’s New? [/h3] 
1. [b]Emojis😎 and Images📷[/b]: Access thousands of emojis directly in chat!
2. [b]Images[/b]: Upload and send your own images!
3. [b]Links🔗[/b]: Links you type in chat become clickable.
5. [b]Autocomplete🏷️[/b]: Type any command OR tag prefix to autocomplete [url=https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Chat#Tags] tags. [/url]
5. [b]Autocomplete Tags And Commands🏷️[/b]: Type any tag prefix to autocomplete [url=https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Chat#Tags] tags. [/url]
6. [b]More✍️[/b]: A better text editor, customizable scrollbar, and much more!
