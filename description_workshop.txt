[h1] Chat+ [/h1] 
Chat+ is a massive overhaul to Terraria's chat system — enhancing both its appearance and functionality with powerful new features.

[h3] ✨ What’s New? [/h3] 
1. [b]Emojis😎[/b]: Access thousands of emojis directly in chat!
2. [b]Images📷[/b]: Upload and send your own images!
3. [b]Links🔗[/b]: All links in chat become instantly clickable.
4. [b]Autocomplete!🏷️[/b]: Type any command or tag prefix to autocomplete [url=https://terraria.wiki.gg/wiki/Chat#Tags] tags. [/url]
5. [b]Player and mod icons▶️[/b]: Add player icons, mod icons and customize your own player color! 
6. [b]Player and mod stats📊[/b]: A stat viewer is available when hovering over player and mod icons.
7. [b]Text Editor✍️[/b]: Highlight text, copy & paste words, and more!
8. [b]Scrollbar[/b]: Scroll through chat history.

[h3] 🏷️ Autocomplete [/h3] 
Press / to open commands.
Press : to open emojis.
Press @ to open player mentions.
Press # to upload an image.
All other tags have their own prefixes, like [c for colors, [i for items, etc. See all tags with /tags.
